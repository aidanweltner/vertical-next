Waterhouse Row is a community on the banks of the Boise River, the edge of the Greenbelt and in the heart of the Waterfront District near downtown Boise.

Beautifully designed town homes and commercial spaces overlook the Boise River, Esther Simplot Park, Boise Whitewater Park, Quinn's Pond and the Boise Greenbelt. The project was completed in 2019. [Phase II](/project/waterhouse-row-ii) of the development is slated for completion in 2022.
